Shared Accomodation Calculator Digital Nomads

ℹ
The calculator takes into account each nomad's stay duration and room type preference, applying different weights to room types: Large Room (1.5), Medium Room (1), and Small Room (0.75). The total cost is divided among nomads based on these factors, with higher weights resulting in a larger share of the cost. Any additional shared costs are distributed evenly among the nomads.

Instructions

  1. Enter the number of nomads who will share the accommodation.
  2. For each nomad, enter their name, duration of stay (in days), and room type preference.
  3. Input the total accommodation cost (in euros) for the entire stay.
  4. Enter any additional shared costs (in euros) that should be included in the cost calculation. For example, utility bills or cleaning fees.
  5. Click the "Calculate Costs" button to submit the form and calculate the individual costs for each nomad.
  6. Review the "Cost Summary" section to see the calculated costs for each nomad.
